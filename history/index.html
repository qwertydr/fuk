About Us

YRJCite is a Youth Research Journal in-house build citation generator.

YRJ releases significant research papers each year. Hosting the Graduate and High School Research programs is YRJ's main initiative. Visit to learn more!

To encourage newcomers to do research and foster their intellectual development, YRJ organizes an International Academic Research Competition each year for high school and university to any age group in any discipline.
